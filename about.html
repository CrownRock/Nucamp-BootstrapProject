Though our hearts are ever Tuscan, we love our home in Fort Collins!

Food and family are very important in our lives. It is with great passion we follow our family's tradition in restaurant service. We still cook as we did in Italy, using the finest produce and proteins from local farms. Everything we serve is made fresh daily for the finest flavor, texture and presentation.

We have a large cellar with wine from around the world. We're proud to feature fine Italian wines, some of which are made in our own vineyard near Radda, Chianti. We invite you to come see for youselves the quality of wine we present, you're most welcome at our table. Mangia, Mangia!

We're proud to be members of the Fort Collins community

From the schools to the fabulous moutains, rivers and lakes, Fort Collins is a great home for our kids. We love the outdoor activities as well as the many social events here.

We moved here in 1998. With support from our family in Italy we opened Villa Francesca in Old Town in 1999 and have enjoyed tremendous support from the community. We're honored that the community enjoys our restaurant so much!

When our family in Chianti visits they are so impressed, both with our success and with FOCO people who are as friendly as those in their home town. Please visit us in Old Town and see for yourself.
